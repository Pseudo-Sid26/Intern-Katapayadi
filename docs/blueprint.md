# **App Name**: Indi-Puzzler

## Core Features:

- Oracle Hints: Oracle's Hint System: Generates riddles or trivia related to the solution of a given Katapayadi puzzle, acting as a helpful tool when users are stuck. Uses an LLM tool to decide on relevant trivia.
- Dynasty Ledger: Dynasty Ledger: A digital leaderboard that tracks the collective scores of players aligned with different Indian dynasties, promoting long-term engagement and competition.
- Artifact Viewer: Artifact Vault: A personal gallery displaying 3D models of collectibles earned, allowing players to explore and learn about historical artifacts from various regions of India.
- Map Scanner: Regional Map Fragments: Integrates physical jigsaw-style map pieces with QR codes that unlock digital puzzles and content, enhancing the DigiPhysical experience.
- Sutra Unlock: Sutra Scroll Cases: Physical lockboxes that are opened with combinations earned by solving digital puzzles, providing a tangible reward and enhancing the escape room feel of the DigiPhysical experiences.
- Puzzles: Puzzle Interface: Display the Katapayadi puzzles for solving. User interaction to solve puzzles. Feedback on answers given. Integration with hint system.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Saffron (#FF9933), representing courage, sacrifice, and renunciation, evoking the historical and cultural significance of India.
- Background color: Desaturated saffron (#F2E6D9), creating a warm and inviting canvas.
- Accent color: Indigo (#4B0082), symbolizing depth, mystery, and the night sky, drawing from the intellectual and spiritual heritage of India.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif, for readability and a modern, warm aesthetic.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', sans-serif, lending an elegant, fashionable, high-end feel for titles and headings. 'PT Sans' is to be used for blocks of body text.
- Use stylized icons inspired by ancient Indian art and symbols for in-app navigation and features, with a hand-drawn aesthetic.
- A card-based layout will present puzzles and game components, allowing a structured approach to solving puzzles.
